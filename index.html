About

More than just a café finder.

Your Daily Pour is built for people who take their coffee seriously. Not just where to get a flat white — but who is roasting the beans, which milk brands they use, and whether the experience matches the cup.

Browse nearby cafés filtered to your exact taste. Discover places that use your favourite roaster, stock your preferred milk brand, and serve the brew style you love. Read honest reviews from people who care as much about coffee as you do.

Found a great café we don't have yet? Submit it. We review every listing before it goes live — so you can trust what you find.

How It Works

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who We Are

Your Daily Pour ("we", "us", "our") operates the Your Daily Pour mobile application. We are based in New Zealand. For privacy-related questions, contact us at: yourdailypour2026@gmail.com

What Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Name and email address when you register
  • Profile photo and bio if you choose to add them
  • Café submissions including name, address, photos, and details
  • Reviews, ratings, comments, and photos you post

Information collected automatically:

  • Your device location (only when you use the Nearby feature, and only with your permission)
  • App usage data for improving the service

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate your account and provide the app's features
  • To show nearby cafés based on your location
  • To display your reviews and profile to other users
  • To review and approve café submissions
  • To improve the app

How We Store Your Information

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure. Firebase complies with international data protection standards. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Location Data

Location is only accessed when you use the Nearby feature. We do not track or store your location history. You can deny location permission at any time in your device settings — the app will still work, but the Nearby feature will be unavailable.

Photos

Photos you upload are stored securely on Google Firebase Storage. By uploading a photo you confirm you have the right to share it.

User-Generated Content

Reviews, ratings, and café information you submit may be visible to all users of the app. Do not post content you do not want to be publicly visible.

Your Rights (New Zealand Privacy Act 2020)

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, email us at yourdailypour2026@gmail.com

Children

Your Daily Pour is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app.
